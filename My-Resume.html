Hello, I'm Tirth

Transforming Real Moments Into Timeless Films

I'm a professional wedding and event video editor with a passion for visual storytelling that goes beyond just transitions and effects. I specialize in crafting cinematic edits for weddings, pre-weddings, birthdays, and cultural events—using software like Davinci Resolve, Adobe Premiere Pro, and After Effects. Every frame is carefully curated to reflect raw emotions, authentic moments, and the beauty of your celebration.

To ensure unmatched quality and efficiency, I integrate AI tools into my editing pipeline. From AI-based music syncing and auto-color grading to scene detection and slow-motion enhancement using Topaz Video AI, I combine creativity with cutting-edge tech. This allows me to deliver high-quality films faster—without compromising on the storytelling or visual tone.

Whether it's a grand destination wedding or a cozy engagement ceremony, I understand the nuances of Indian traditions and modern aesthetics. My goal is to edit every project with emotional depth and visual polish, customized for social sharing, reels, or cinematic showcases. With a client-first approach and detail-driven process, I bring memories to life—frame by frame.
